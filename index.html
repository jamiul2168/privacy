Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

JH G-Drive Manage values your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

1. Data Collection & Access

Our app accesses your Google Drive files only to perform actions requested by you (List, Rename, Search, and Delete). We do not store any of your files or personal data on our own servers.

2. Use of Google User Data

We use your Google profile information (Name and Email) only to identify your account within the app interface. We do not share this data with any third parties.

3. Data Security

All data transmission between the app and Google Drive is encrypted using Google’s official OAuth 2.0 protocol. Your Google password is never accessible to us.

4. Third-Party Access

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your Drive data to outside parties. Your data remains entirely within the Google ecosystem.
